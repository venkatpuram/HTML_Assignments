Masala egg Bhurji Recipe

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • oil
  • masala
  • green chillies
  • salt
  • chilli powder
  • onions
  • turmeric powder

procedure

  1. Take a pan and pour oil in it
  2. Add green chillies to the pan
  3. Stir them well until golden brown
  4. chopped onions to the pan and saute together
  5. Add salt, turmeric powder, chilli powder, masala and mix them well
  6. Now add the eggs to the pan and whisk them thoroughly until cooked
  7. Serve hot
